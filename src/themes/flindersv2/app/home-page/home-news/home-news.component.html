Flinders Academic Commons

Administered by Flinders University Library, the Flinders Academic Commons (FAC) is Flinders University’s former institutional repository. It is now closed to user submissions, except for DOI provisioning. ResearchNow is the current repository for Flinders University’s research outputs. Flinders University Research Data repository is available via ROADS (Repository of Open Access DataSets). For submission queries or issues, please contact dspace@flinders.edu.au
